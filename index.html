Apex legends is a fps (first person shooter) battle royale. Before you begin the game you can pick one of the 19 unique characters. Every character has their own passive, tactical and ultimate ability that you can use yo your own advaage .In apex legends you start a game and you begin in a flying ship. Once you have jummped out of the ship you fly to the place you want to go on the island and collect your loot (weapons, attachments, healing, ammo and much more).

Apex legends was released the 4th of february 2019. The game was released by EA and made by respawn entertainment. Despite Respawn's timid expectations, Apex Legends hit 1 million players within the first 24 hours of release, back on February 4, 2019. Then, by the end of the first week, it had reached 25 million players, and then 50 million in the first month.

Apex legends is in season 11 at the moment. It started with season 0 also known as the preseason, when the game released. Then the other seasons came and each season came along with new weapons legends and sometimes even maps.
