Apex legends is a fps (first person shooter) battle royale. Before you begin the game you can pick one of the 19 unique characters. Every character has their own passive, tactical and ultimate ability that you can use yo your own advaage .In apex legends you start a game and you begin in a flying ship. Once you have jummped out of the ship you fly to the place you want to go on the island and collect your loot (weapons, attachments, healing, ammo and much more).

Apex legends was released the 4th of february 2019. The game was released by EA and made by respawn entertainment.
