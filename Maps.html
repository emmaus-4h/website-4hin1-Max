Four big Battle Royale.svg Battle Royale maps: Kings Canyon (from launch); World's Edge (from Season 3.svg Season 3); Olympus (from Season 7.svg Season 7); and Storm Point (from Season 11.svg Season 11). Four small Arenas Icon.svg Arenas maps: Party Crasher and Phase Runner (from Season 9.svg Season 9); Overflow (from the Thrillseekers Icon.svg Thrillseekers event); and Encore (from the Monsters Within Icon.svg Monsters Within event). For Battle Royale, at the start of each season, either a new map will be added, or one of the current maps will receive a map update (MU). Minor map changes, called Town Takeovers (TT), occur sparingly and they come with events. Town Takeovers are modeled after a Legend.
